## Purpose of the Study

You are invited to participate in a research study evaluating the usability and functionality of a mobile application designed to measure environmental noise levels on the George Mason University campus.

The purpose of this study is to:
1. Test the app's accuracy in measuring noise levels
2. Evaluate the user interface and user experience
3. Identify bugs and areas for improvement
4. Gather feedback on features and design

This research is being conducted as part of an academic course requirement.

---

## Study Procedures

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:

1. **Testing Session (30 minutes total):**
   - Walk to 3 different campus locations with the researcher
   - Use a mobile app to measure noise levels at each location
   - Complete 6 simple tasks guided by the researcher
   - Share your thoughts out loud as you use the app

2. **Survey (5 minutes):**
   - Complete a short questionnaire about your experience
   - Answer questions about the app's usability
   - Provide open-ended feedback

**Total Time Commitment:** Approximately 30-35 minutes

**Location:** George Mason University campus (outdoor and indoor public areas)

## Confidentiality

Your privacy will be protected to the maximum extent allowable by law:

1. **Data Storage:**
   - Your responses will be kept confidential
   - Data will be stored securely on password-protected devices
   - Only research team members will have access to identified data

2. **Anonymization:**
   - You will be assigned a participant ID (e.g., P001, P002)
   - Published results will use only anonymized, aggregated data
   - No personally identifiable information will be published

3. **Data Retention:**
   - Research data will be kept for the duration of the semester
   - Data will be deleted after final project submission (Week 14)
   - Consent forms will be stored separately from response data

4. **Audio/Video:**
   - No audio or video recording will be conducted without your explicit permission
   - If we request to take photos (of you using the app), you can decline
   - Photos will only be used for academic presentation purposes
