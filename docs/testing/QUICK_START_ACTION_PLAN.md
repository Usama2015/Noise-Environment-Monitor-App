## ğŸ’¡ **Pro Tips**

1. **Start recruiting TODAY** - The earlier you start, the more options you have
2. **Over-recruit** - Aim for 10 sign-ups to get 6-8 completed sessions (account for no-shows)
3. **Test the app yourself FIRST** - Walk through all 6 tasks before testing with participants
4. **Scout locations in advance** - Don't assume a place will be quiet/noisy
5. **Print extra materials** - Always have 2-3 extra copies of everything
6. **Arrive early** - 15 minutes before first session to set up
7. **Stay neutral** - Don't defend the app, just observe and listen
8. **Take notes** - Write down observations immediately after each session
